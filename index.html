About Me

I’m a markets-focused builder working at the intersection of finance, technology, and strategy. At Barclays, I design tools and analytics that support trading and investment decisions across FX and Rates.

I graduated from IIT Gandhinagar with a background in engineering, but my work today focuses on how systems, incentives, and human behavior interact in real-world decision-making environments.

Across my career, I’ve gravitated toward roles that require both structured thinking and execution under pressure — whether on trading floors or in competitive sport, where I represented my institute at a national level and earned a gold medal for excellence.

I’m particularly interested in building scalable decision systems and pursuing long-term roles in investing and strategy.

What I Build

I build systems that convert complexity into clarity — enabling faster, more informed decisions in high-stakes environments.

Decision Systems for Trading Desks

Designed and deployed real-time analytics platforms that integrate macroeconomic data, funding signals, and market structure insights. These tools enable traders to identify opportunities, manage risk, and act with speed and precision.

Liquidity & Funding Intelligence

Built dashboards tracking money market flows, asset swap spreads, and funding dynamics — improving visibility into short-term liquidity conditions and their impact on trading strategies.

TradeTracker — Investment Decision Platform

Built from scratch, TradeTracker is a centralized platform to structure, evaluate, and track trade ideas across desks. It standardizes how investment theses are articulated, compared, and reviewed — forming the foundation for a more disciplined, scalable capital allocation process.

The platform is now being integrated into an investment committee framework, improving transparency and decision quality across teams.
